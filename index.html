Profile

My scholarly goal is to realize sustainable manufacturing by enabling high-performance maintenance operations in digitalized environments. My vision is to renew the maintenance field through ground-breaking research in close cooperation with industry.

I combine research excellence with industrial impact. My work spans empirical research methodology, practical engineering problem-solving, and social-science-informed analysis of industrial organizations. I have experience in producing disruptive research results, leading innovative research projects, and delivering knowledge, tools, and methods that inform theory, practice, and policy.

Research grants and projects

I have led and contributed to research projects in roles including project leader, grant holder, grant proposal author, operational manager, main investigator, researcher, educational developer, and PhD supervisor.

Maintenance of Battery Production at Industrial Scale (MATTER@SCALE), 2023–2026

Project leader, grant holder

Partners: Chalmers, KTH, Volvo AB, Volvo Cars, Scania, Northvolt. Develops and scales key maintenance enablers for circularity in the Swedish battery industry.

Total budget: 10.5 MSEK; Chalmers funding: 3.5 MSEK.

Maintenance of Battery Production (MATTER), 2022–2023

Project leader, grant holder

Established the foundation for research on maintenance of battery production and sustainable battery industry development in Sweden.

Total budget: 1.1 MSEK; Chalmers funding: 0.4 MSEK.

Disturbance Data Fusion (DFusion), 2021–2024

Research design and PhD supervision

Explored digital tools for fusion and analysis of production-system data to improve disturbance management, efficiency, and waste reduction.

Total budget: 12 MSEK; Chalmers funding: 2.9 MSEK.

Smart Maintenance in Energy Production (STEP2), 2020–2022

Lead operational researcher and project administration

Implemented and evaluated Smart Maintenance in energy production, including organizational assessment and commercial software implementation.

Total budget and Chalmers funding: 3.45 MSEK.

Sustainability, Smart Maintenance and Factory Design Testbed (SUMMIT), 2017–2021

Researcher and educational developer

Developed demonstrator testbeds for preventive maintenance, factory design, reduced emissions, better work environments, and inclusive factory design.

Total budget: 21 MSEK; Chalmers funding: 4.3 MSEK.

Smart Maintenance Assessment (SMASh), 2017–2019

Project design, consortium building, WP leader, lead researcher

Conceptualized Smart Maintenance, developed a measurement instrument, tested performance implications, and developed roadmaps and digital services.

Total budget: 5.6 MSEK; Chalmers funding: 2.3 MSEK.
